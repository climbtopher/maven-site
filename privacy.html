Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Maven is operated by Morningside Design LLC ("Maven," "we," "us," or "our").

Maven is a family logistics assistant. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have. We built Maven to be a trustworthy place for your family's information, and we collect only what we need to do that job.

Information we collect

  • Email content (read-only, transient). With your explicit authorization, Maven reads incoming family email through the Gmail API to extract logistics information. Raw email content is deleted within 60 seconds after extraction and is never stored permanently.
  • Extracted event data. Event titles, dates, times, and locations derived from your email and other connected sources.
  • Family member names and roles. The people in your family and their roles (for example, primary, co-manager, or caregiver).
  • Child activity information. Logistics details about children's activities and schedules, provided by a parent or guardian.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not store email bodies permanently — raw email content is deleted within 60 seconds of processing.
  • We do not collect or store your passwords.
  • We do not collect or store payment or card data beyond what our payment processor, Stripe, handles on our behalf.

How we use your information

We use the information above for a single purpose: to extract your family's schedule information and surface it in the Maven app so your household can coordinate. We do not sell your data. We do not use your data to train AI models.

Third parties

Maven relies on a small number of carefully chosen service providers to deliver the service:

  • Google (Gmail API) — read-only access to the email you authorize, used solely to extract logistics information.
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — used to interpret extracted text under a Zero Data Retention arrangement; your data is not used to train models.
  • Google (Gemini AI) — used to interpret extracted text under a Zero Data Retention arrangement; your data is not used to train models.
  • Stripe — used for billing only; Stripe handles payment information directly.
Before any information is sent to an AI provider, children's names are replaced with anonymized tokens and are never transmitted in plain text. If anonymization cannot be applied, the request is blocked rather than sent.

Children's data

Maven is designed to be COPPA-compliant. Information about children is provided by their parent or legal guardian, who controls and consents to its use. We store only logistics data — schedules and activities — and we never collect or store clinical or health data about children.

Data retention

Extracted events are retained for up to 2 years, or until you delete your account, whichever comes first. Raw email content is deleted within 60 seconds of processing and is never retained.

Your rights

You may request deletion of all of your family's data at any time by emailing privacy@morningsidedesign.com. We will process verified deletion requests promptly.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to privacy@morningsidedesign.com.
